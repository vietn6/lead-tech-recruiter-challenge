Context

AirOps is an AI workflow orchestration platform that exposes data capabilities directly to our customers. Today, we have one data scientist on the team.

A core belief here is that data is our strategic moat. As we build agents, their usefulness and defensibility comes from plugging into our proprietary data and data infrastructure — not just the underlying model.

We need to build out our data engineering, data science, and machine learning functions to support a consumer-facing data product.

Your challenge

Recommend a hiring strategy, select the ideal candidates, and provide outreach to each person you'd prioritize. The three sections below define exactly what we're looking for.

Section Three

Outreach

Draft personalized outreach messages to three of the candidates you've identified — one per function. These aren't templates. Each message should show that you've actually thought about this person.

Show you understand their background — something specific, not generic praise.
